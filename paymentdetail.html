DETAILS AND STEPS FOR DONATING


The value of life is not in its duration, but in its donation.

  • Enter the amount you wish to donate.
  • Fill up your mail ID and mobile number for reciving your recipt after successful donation. Then click pay Now.
  • It will ask for mode of payment, choose the desire one and proceed.
  • After choosing the mode fill up the details asked for.
  • Atlast click Success for completing the payment or Failure to stop.
  • Once you press Success you will be redirected with Payment Success message and a mail will be sent to you with your payment recipt.
